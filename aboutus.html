About Us

Official website of Frankyteer which serves as a part of the Khasi Hills Archery Sports Institute based in Shillong Meghalaya. Its a local arcades regulated by the govt of Meghalaya which is played by shooting of arrows at a marked target. This arcade has been going since the 80's and is primarily held in polo ground shillong. It begins by shooting of arrows by 30 to 50 shooters with a total of 500 to 600 arrows at a given target and the total arrows that hit the target is counted and the results is the last two digits of the total number of arrows that landed on the target.
