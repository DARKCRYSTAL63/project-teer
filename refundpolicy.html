Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025 16:46:43

FrankyTeer believes in helping its customers as far as possible, and has therefore a liberal cancellation policy.

Cancellation Policy

  • Cancellations will be considered only if the request is made immediately after placing the order.
  • The cancellation request will not be entertained for wrong input order by the users.

Refund Policy

  • Refund can be made if the customer's cancelation is approved.
  • No refund without booking receipt.
  • The request will be entertained once we have checked and determined the same at our end.

Refund Processing

  • In case of any Refunds approved by Frankyteer, it'll take 1-2 days for the refund to be processed to the end customer.

For any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact our customer service team.
